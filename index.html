About The Event

Get ready for the ultimate display of skill, speed, and style! The Midnight Slide brings together the best drivers in Los Santos for a night of high-octane drifting action. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a spectator looking for thrills, this is an event you won't want to miss!

Join us at the **Sandy Shores Airfield** for an unforgettable evening. Prepare your ride, hone your skills, and get ready to leave your mark on the tarmac.

Event Schedule

1st

Driver Check-in & Warm-up
Get signed up, meet fellow drivers, and take some warm-up laps on the practice track. Judges will be available for questions.

2nd

Main Event: Tandem Drift Competition
The main show! Two drivers, one course, pure synchronized chaos. Judged on proximity, angle, and line.

3rd

Freestyle Drift Showcase
Unleash your individual flair! Drivers have 90 seconds to impress judges with style, difficulty, and car control.

4th

Low-Speed Drifting Challenge
Precision over power. Navigate a tight, technical course at low speeds while maintaining a continuous drift.

5th

Awards Ceremony & Wrap-up
Trophies, prizes, and bragging rights! Celebrate the night's champions and mingle before heading out.

Competitions

Tandem Drift Competition

Drivers pair up to execute synchronized drifts through a challenging course. Judges score on **proximity, angle, and line**. Precision and teamwork are key!

Freestyle Drift Showcase

Show off your individual skill! Each driver gets 90 seconds to perform their most creative and impressive solo drift routine. Judged on **style, difficulty, and car control**.

Low-Speed Drifting Challenge

A test of ultimate car control. Navigate a tight, technical course at low speeds, maintaining a continuous, controlled drift. Hit a cone, you're out!

Full rules for each competition will be available at check-in.
